Recreation & Parks

Pools, playing fields, summer camps, and year-round programs for residents of every age across the City's parks and facilities.

Summer 2026 is here

Both city pools are open for the season, registration for summer programs is underway, and the StoryWalk trail has a fresh title posted along the Bryant–Mamaroneck path. Residents can register online or in person at the Recreation office in City Hall.

Our parks

White Plains maintains more than a dozen parks and green spaces, including Tibbits Park downtown, Delfino Park's athletic fields, and the Bryant–Mamaroneck StoryWalk trail, where a new children's book is posted page by page along the walking path each season.

  • Tibbits Park — downtown green with the summer concert series
  • Delfino Park — ball fields, tennis courts, and a skate park
  • Gardella Park — pool, playground, and picnic shelters
  • Battle Hill — open lawns and historic Revolutionary War markers
