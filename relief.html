Senior & veteran relief

Targeted property-tax relief and assistance for older adults and those who served. Here's what's available and how to get help applying.

For older adults (65+)

Senior Citizens exemption

A reduction in assessed value for income-eligible owners aged 65 and older.

Enhanced STAR

A larger school-tax benefit for seniors who meet the State income limit.

Payment plans

Spread a tax or utility balance into manageable installments.

For veterans

Alternative Veterans exemption

For veterans who served during a designated period of war, with added relief for service-connected disability.

Cold War Veterans exemption

For those who served during the Cold War era and meet the requirements.

Eligible Funds exemption

For veterans who used eligible funds (pension, bonus, or insurance) to buy a home.

Frequently asked

Can a senior who is also a veteran get both?

Often yes. Senior and veteran benefits apply to different parts of your tax bill and can frequently be combined. Staff will confirm based on your situation.

Is there income-based help if I don't qualify for an exemption?

Payment plans are available to anyone with a balance, and staff can point you to county and state programs that may help.
