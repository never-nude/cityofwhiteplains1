Holiday schedule

When a holiday falls on or before your normal collection day in the same week, that pickup and the rest of the week shift one day later. Observed holidays:

  • Independence Day — Saturday, July 4, 2026
  • Labor Day — Monday, September 7, 2026
  • Thanksgiving — Thursday, November 26, 2026
  • Christmas Day — Friday, December 25, 2026
  • New Year's Day — Friday, January 1, 2027

What goes where

Keep recycling clean, empty, and dry. When in doubt, leave it out of recycling.

  • Recycling: paper, cardboard (flattened), glass bottles, metal cans, and rigid plastics #1–#2.
  • Refuse: non-recyclable household waste in closed containers.
  • Organics & yard waste: leaves and brush per the seasonal program; no plastic bags.
  • Not at the curb: electronics, batteries, paint, and hazardous waste — use a drop-off or special pickup.
