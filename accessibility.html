Accessibility

The City of White Plains is committed to ensuring that everyone — including people with disabilities — can access the information and services on this website.

Our commitment

We want every resident to be able to find what they need here, regardless of ability or the technology they use. This site is designed and maintained to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA — the standard referenced by Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act and the Americans with Disabilities Act.

What we've built in

Accessibility is part of how the site is put together, not an afterthought. That includes:

  • Clear heading structure and landmark regions so screen readers can navigate each page.
  • A “Skip to main content” link at the top of every page for keyboard users.
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images and icons, and decorative icons hidden from assistive technology.
  • Color combinations chosen to meet AA contrast ratios, with information never conveyed by color alone.
  • Full keyboard operability, with visible focus indicators on links, buttons, and form fields.
  • Responsive layouts that reflow cleanly when text is enlarged up to 200%.
  • A full English and Spanish experience, selectable from the top of every page.

Adjusting your experience

Your browser and device offer built-in tools that work throughout this site: zoom to enlarge text, high-contrast or dark modes, and built-in screen readers such as VoiceOver (Apple) and Narrator (Windows). Most pages remain fully usable with text scaled well beyond the default size.

Requesting an accommodation

If you need information from this site in an alternate format — large print, audio, or another accessible format — or if you need an accommodation to participate in a City program, meeting, or service, the City will work with you to provide it. Reasonable-accommodation requests for public meetings should be made as far in advance as possible.

Tell us about a barrier

If you run into a page, document, or feature that is difficult to use, we want to know. Please tell us the page address, what happened, and the assistive technology or browser you were using, and we will work to fix it and get you the information you need in the meantime.
