City Government

Government & transparency

How White Plains governs itself: the Mayor and Common Council, public meetings and records, the budget, and the laws that apply across the City.

Elected officials

White Plains is led by an elected Mayor and a seven-member Common Council. Mayor Justin C. Brasch heads the executive branch; the Common Council, led by Council President John M. Martin, is the City's legislative body.
