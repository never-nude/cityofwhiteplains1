About the City calendar

Common Council regular meetings are generally held on the first Monday of each month at 7:30 p.m. in the Common Council Chambers, with work sessions and committee meetings scheduled as needed. Public hearings are noticed in advance and are open to all residents.

City offices are closed on the legal holidays listed here, which also shift trash and recycling collection. Recreation programs, library events, and community happenings round out the rest of the calendar.
